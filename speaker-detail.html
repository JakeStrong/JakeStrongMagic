Jake Strong

Ever since I was old enough to speak, I’ve been obsessed with magic. Recently, I’ve taken my obsession to the next level and have made a career out of my hobby. Since then, I have been fortunate enough to perform hundreds of shows all over the Tri-State area. In 2016, I was one of six “2016 Society of American Magicians Star of Tomorrow” in America. I have also lent my talents to charity including the Ronald McDonald house, many schools, and a self-made successful fundraiser raising money for a family in need.
